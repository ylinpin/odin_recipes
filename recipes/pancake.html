Banana pancakes

an easy way to make perfect banana pancakes!

source: allrecipes

ingredients

  • flour
  • sugar
  • baking powder
  • an egg
  • salt
  • milk
  • vegetable oil
  • banana

Steps

  1. Mix: Combine your dry ingredients (flour, sugar, salt, baking powder) in one bowl and your wet ingredients (egg, milk, vegetable oil, mashed bananas) in another bowl. Add the dry ingredients to the bowl with the wet ingredients, then stir until they're incorporated.
  2. Cook: Pour the batter in Â¼ cup portions onto a lightly oiled pan or griddle over medium-high heat. Cook for a few minutes, flip with a spatula, and cook for another few minutes (or until each side is golden brown).
  3. Serve: Serve your banana pancakes immediately. They're delicious alone or with your favorite pancake toppings.
